Frequently asked questions

What counts as an invoice?

Any vendor bill processed through Ledgerly — captured via email, upload, vendor portal, or API.

Can I switch plans?

Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the next billing cycle. Prorated either way.

Are there per-transaction fees?

ACH and checks are included on every paid plan. Virtual cards earn rebates. International wires charged at cost.

Do you offer a free trial?

The Free plan is permanent — start there and upgrade when your volume outgrows it.

Need something custom?

Multi-entity rollouts, custom approval logic, or procurement-friendly contracts. Tell us what you need.
